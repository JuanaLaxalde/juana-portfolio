The Help

The Help is a powerful drama set in 1960s Mississippi that follows aspiring writer Skeeter Phelan as she interviews Black maids to document their experiences working for white families. Through her friendship with Aibileen, a wise and resilient housekeeper, and Minny, a sharp-tongued cook with a troubled past, Skeeter uncovers the harsh realities of racism and injustice in the segregated South. Their courageous collaboration challenges social norms and sparks a quiet revolution, revealing the strength and dignity of the women whose voices had long been ignored.
